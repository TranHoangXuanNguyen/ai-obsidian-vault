# What is AI hallucination?
AI models can generate incorrect or misleading results.
This phenomenon can be caused by the insufficient training data, incorrect assumptions made by the model or biases in the data used to train model.

# How does it occur?
It might occur because of the quality of the data sets used to train models. Another factor is AI just predict the result (or the next token in LLM), so it doesn't actually understand words.
